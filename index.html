Hi, I'm Martin.
A freelance Web Designer and Developer currently living in Berlin.

About:

In 2015 while studying for my degree in Audio Engineering I took up a job working as an intern within a London based tech startup. Within this team I was thrown into the deep end, gaining my first experience applying the knowledge I already had to a professional standard, improving my skills and learning much about the processes involved in creating high quality commercial web applications: server-side development, effective front end and user experience, quality content and design.
This experience sparked my passion to continue learning, expand my skillset and continue work as a web developer

Services and Skills:
